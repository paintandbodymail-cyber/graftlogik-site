FAQ

Questions, answered.

Does it do invoicing or quoting?
No, and that's deliberate. GraftLogik tracks your daily earnings, hours, and materials — that's it. If you want full workshop management with invoicing, CRM, and inventory, there are other apps for that. This one does one thing and does it properly.
Will it work without an internet connection?
Yes. GraftLogik is offline-first. Everything saves to your phone as you work. When you're back online, your data syncs automatically to the cloud for backup. You can lose signal all day and not lose a single job.
What happens after my 30-day free trial?
You'll be prompted to subscribe at £3.99/month or £29.99/year. If you don't subscribe, your data stays safe — but you'll need a subscription to keep tracking new jobs. Cancel any time in your iPhone Settings.
Is there an Android version?
Not yet. Android is on the roadmap for 2026 once iOS is established. Join the mailing list and you'll hear about it first.
Who built this?
Robert Brudenell — a working bodyshop owner based in the UK who got tired of finishing every day guessing what he'd earned. GraftLogik was built in a real workshop, tested on real jobs, and shaped by feedback from real tradespeople.
What trades is this for?
Anyone who bills by the hour. Bodyshop technicians, panel beaters, paint sprayers, SMART repair techs, mobile mechanics, general mechanics, and sole-trader workshop owners. If your job title ends in "-ist", "-ic" or "-er" and you work on the tools, this is for you.
What about my data?
Your data is yours. We don't sell it, share it, or use it for anything other than running the app for you. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
