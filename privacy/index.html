Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 March 2026

GraftLogik ("we", "our", "the app") is a workshop job tracking application. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

What We Collect

  • Account information — email address and shop name, provided during registration
  • Job data — vehicle registrations, job descriptions, time logs, materials, and costings that you enter into the app
  • Usage data — app settings and preferences you configure

Why We Collect It

  • To provide your account and sync your data across devices
  • To calculate earnings, job costs, and daily targets
  • To maintain your settings and preferences

How We Store It

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which runs on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Your data is also cached locally on your device for offline use.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not share your data with third-party advertisers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or photos

Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us
  • You can export your job data by contacting us
  • UK and EU users have additional rights under UK GDPR / EU GDPR including the right to access, rectify, and erase personal data

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.
